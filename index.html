Valorant club

Valorant Club meets every Tuesday and Wednesday after school in X2 from 2:40 to 4:30 pm.

Whether you're looking to play with friends, improve your skills, or just enjoy some solid competition, this is the place to be.
With high-performance computers running Valorant smoothly (100+ fps!), it's perfect for anyone who doesn’t have the gear at home.

Not only will you get the chance to play in a fun, casual environment, but you’ll also have the opportunity to team up with fellow club members for competitive matches, improve your
gameplay, or get some coaching from the experienced players in our school’s esports scene.

Want to take your game to the next level? Queue up with others in the club, form a team, or simply play for fun—either way, there’s always a spot for you.
So, if you’ve got the drive, the competitive spirit, and want to meet others who are just as passionate about Valorant, come talk to Mr. Cresswell!
